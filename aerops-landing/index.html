The Problem

Aerial ag offices run on
WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

Most operators stitch together group chats, paper load sheets and Excel invoices to manage complex multi-aircraft operations. There's a better way.

📱

Job briefs via WhatsApp

Critical spray details lost in chat threads. Pilots guessing at product rates, swath widths and hazard notes at the airstrip.

📋

Paper mixing sheets

Handwritten load sheets, manual calculations, no record of what actually went out. Compliance gaps waiting to happen.

📊

Invoicing from memory

Reconciling tacho times and hectares days after the job. Revenue leaking through forgotten charges and billing delays.

🗂️

No visibility across jobs

Who's flying what, where, when? Multiple aircraft and locations with no single source of truth for the day's plan.

The Platform

Four apps. One operation.

Every role gets their own tailored view — all syncing in real time through the cloud. No more phone calls to find out what's happening.

The Pilot's Field App

Everything the pilot needs at the airstrip — no paperwork, no phone calls.

How It Works

From request to invoice in one system.

AeroOps connects every step of your operation — client, office, pilot and mixer all working from the same live data.

1

Client submits request

Client fills the AeroOps Request form on their phone. It arrives instantly in the Office queue with a reference number.

2

Office reviews & quotes

Dispatcher reviews the job, enters paddock details, auto-calculates an estimate and confirms with the client.

3

Schedule & brief

Drag the job onto the weekly planner. Pilot and mixer apps update instantly — no phone calls needed.

4

Pilot works the job

Pilot taps Start on their app, tracks tacho sessions, completes the job. Record syncs to office automatically.

5

Invoice & done

Office reviews the completion record and pushes a Tax Invoice directly to Xero with one button.
